CHRISTMAS IN JULY SOCIAL GIVEAWAY^

During my social media marketing internship at Royal Credit Union, I planned and conducted monthly social media giveaways to help boost engagement on our accounts and give back to our audience. My favorite giveaway was the Christmas in July giveaway, which we conducted from July 24th-29th, across Facebook, Instagram, Twitter, and Snapchat. Engagement was extremely high for this giveaway, with over 500 comments on our Facebook post and over 350 on Instagram.Our following also increased on each platform: by almost 50 on Snapchat, over 170 on Facebook, and over 200 on Instagram and Twitter. This was a very successful campaign for engagement and followers. I’ve included links to documents with the plan including copy and details for the giveaway as well as a report analyzing results from the giveaway. INFLUENCER MARKETING CAMPAIGN^

During my internship at Royal Credit Union, I helped the marketing team plan and execute their first influencer marketing campaign. This process started with gathering information and research regarding influencers and the type of campaign we wanted to pursue. We decided to do a collaboration with an influencer couple, Sway and Sarah Gonzalez who create DIY home remodeling content mainly on Instagram and TikTok. I collaborated with our team to communicate back and forth with them to secure the partnership, come up with content ideas, and create the final video idea and script/copy. We gave the couple two different options for videos to create so that they had some creative freedom in the process. I also worked with our compliance and legal teams to ensure that we were following all legal standards, and we created a contract together for the influencer agreement. Unfortunately, my internship ended shortly after the contract was signed, but I have included the partnership video below as well as some documents from the planning process.
